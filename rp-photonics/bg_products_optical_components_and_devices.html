Fiber Optics Software

RP Fiber Calculator – a convenient tool for calculations on optical fibers:

  • Define index or doping profiles with the graphical user interface.
  • Calculate the guided modes and all their properties.

  • Calculate how much power of a laser beam is launched into the modes.
  • Simulate light propagation within the fiber and coupling of light from one fiber to another.

The basic version of this software is offered for free!
