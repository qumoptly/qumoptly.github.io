The Company – Basic Facts

RP Photonics Consulting GmbH is an independent and privately owned company, providing competent technical consulting services in the area of photonics. The topical areas include lasers and amplifiers, nonlinear optics, fiber optics, ultrashort pulses, multilayer optics, fluctuations and noise. The main customer groups are

  • manufacturers and users of lasers and other modern optical equipment
  • investors and funding agencies
  • communications and advertising experts

RP Photonics is owned and run by the founder himself, Dr. Rüdiger Paschotta. He is committed to highest professional standards in all respects, and regularly receives a very high level of customer satisfaction.

RP Photonics does not buy or sell any hardware (e.g. lasers and optics), and gives independent advice concerning such hardware. The company does not run a laser laboratory, but has various contacts with companies and laboratories where joint development projects including the fabrication of prototypes are possible.

Note that RP Photonics Consulting GmbH is not recruiting staff. (No applications please!)
