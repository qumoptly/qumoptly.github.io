Distributors for Software from RP Photonics

In some countries, software from RP Photonics can also be obtained via distributors. In any case, delivery and support will be done by Dr. Paschotta of RP Photonics, the developer of the software.

Never buy software with unclear origin!

Please note: Never buy our software through any company which is neither listed as a distributor on this page nor otherwise explicitly named by us as an authorized supplier. Otherwise, you may obtain an illegal product which may be infected with any conceivable malware and for which of course no technical support is available. If you learn about any doubtful offers, please inform us about them.

USA: DPM Photonics

RP Photonics is cooperating with DPM Photonics, a U.S. company, concerning software sales. DPM Photonics may sell worldwide.

China: ASDOptics

Since 10/2012, RP Photonics is also cooperating with ASDOPTICS in Wuhan, China. This means that Chinese customers are asked to place their software orders via ASDOPTICS.

Please note that ASDOPTICS is the only authorized distributor of RP Photonics in China, except that DPM Photonics in the U.S. (see above) has the right to sell worldwide. Do not order from any other company in China, as some illegitimate sale will not give you the real RP Photonics software and its technical support, and your money would be lost.

Again, delivery and support will in any case be done directly by Dr. Paschotta of RP Photonics, the developer of the software.

Japan: Kokyo

Since 06/2016, Kokyo is the official distributor for software from RP Photonics in Japan.

South Korea: Luen Soft

Since 04/2017, Luen Soft is the official distributor for software from RP Photonics in South Korea.
