Newsletters from RP Photonics

RP Photonics publishes the following blogs, for which free newsletters (or call them blog letters!) are available:

  • The Photonics Spotlight is a blog on photonics and laser technology, which is associated with the Encyclopedia of Laser Physics and Technology. It focuses on the scientific and technological aspects in photonics, rather than on business news.
  • The RP Photonics Software News present information on our simulation and design software. It is useful both for users of that software and for those who want to learn more about it.

Both appear roughly once per month – sometimes a bit more often, but you will surely not be flooded with mails.

By the way, you may use RSS feeds instead of e-mail delivery: Photonics Spotlight | RP Photonics Software News

Note: our newsletters are used for spreading useful information, not just advertising messages!
