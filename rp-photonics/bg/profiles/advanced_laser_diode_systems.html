Company Description

Advanced Laser Diode Systems A.L.S. GmbH was founded in 2002 and is a leading manufacturer of picosecond semiconductor laser diode systems called PiLas. The laser diodes are driven in gain-switched operation to emit short optical pulses. Scientists, engineers and technical staff at A.L.S. have a longstanding experience and know-how that allow them to push this laser technology to produce the shortest possible optical pulses down to 20 ps.

A.L.S. optimizes every laser head to meet the customer requirements regarding wavelength, pulse duration, pulse energy, average power, peak power, pulse shape, spectral width, polarization, etc. The customer is also advised for beam delivery options to optimize the optical setup at the customer side. A broad choice of wavelengths, from 375 nm to 2 μm, and the continuous repetition rate tunability by internal or external triggering, allow a wide use of these laser systems for analysis and testing of surfaces, materials and detectors.

Advanced Laser Diode Systems is a German based company located in the Photonics-Center in Berlin-Adlershof. It belongs to the Swiss based company Onefive GmbH.
