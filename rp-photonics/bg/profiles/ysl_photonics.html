Company Description

Advances in photonics technology enable progress in health, mobility and sustainability.

YSL Photonics explores, develops and manufactures next-generation of supercontinuum source (supercontimuum laser, white light source, white light laser), picosecond pulsed and femtosecond fiber laser that bring new capabilities, higher reliability and

lower cost of ownership to a diverse range of industrial and R & D applications. The core team has combined of more than 100 years of track record of award-winning products and successful business in photonics.
