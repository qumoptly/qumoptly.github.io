Company Description

IDIL Fibres Optiques is a leading supplier of lasers and optical fiber technologies.

IDIL Fibres Optiques specialises in the design development, manufacturing and distribution of fiber systems intended for use in the science, defence and industry markets.

Our products portfolio focuses on optical fiber technology, including passive and active optical components, fiber devices, speciality fibers, fiber-optic sensors and fiber lasers. Besides, we offer spectrometers, cameras and didactic products for teaching in the fields of fiber optics and telecommunications technologies.

IDIL is involved in prototype creations as well as small to medium mass production.

IDIL masters numerous technologies and performs all steps of the development process.

IDIL is thus able to design and to manufacture all sub-assemblies (optical, mechanical, electronic, software) in-house.
