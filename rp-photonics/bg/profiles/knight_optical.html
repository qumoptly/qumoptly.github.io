homogenizing rods

infrared optics

Knight Optical supply a wide range of IR optics, stock and custom, for use in the infrared wavebands. Optical and mechanical components that are available as stock items or talk to us about your custom IR requirements – all QA assured.

laser mirrors

Laser Mirrors – reflective coatings optimised for >1 μm on a precision flat substrate and a high damage threshold – perfect for high-power lasers.

lenses

Knight Optical offers a wide range of high quality lenses for a variety of applications, from aspheric lenses to correct spherical aberrations, to thin Fresnel lenses and large condenser lenses. We can supply from stock or bespoke, in a range of materials to suit and where necessary we can edge existing stock lenses for a fast turnaround, all QA assured.

mirrors

Knight Optical supplies a complete range of stock and custom precision mirrors for use as reflectors across a wide waveband, UV to NIR (<0.2 to >10 μm). All our custom optics are checked for quality in our state-of-the-art metrology laboratory allowing us to work to the highest quality assurance.

optical filters

Knight Optical's stock and custom optical filters are utilized in a massive range of optical systems, from ARPN systems to fluoresence microsopy, from gas detection to lighting design. All optics are quality-assured.

optical windows

Optical Windows – precision grade windows from stock or custom for use in the UV/VIS/NIR and available at <λ/10, making them perfect for laser applications.

parabolic mirrors

Knight Optical supplies a range of stock and custom optical windows in a selection of materials to meet all scientific instrument applications from UV to visible and NIR wavelenghts. All filters are quality-assured.

plastic optics

Most of the single lens forms, planoconvex, planoconcave, meniscus and aspheric lenses are available in plastic. Plastic lenses are generally cheaper and lighter than glass equivalents but of lower quality. Also we have plastic screen diffusers. Take a look at our full range both stock and custom optics.
