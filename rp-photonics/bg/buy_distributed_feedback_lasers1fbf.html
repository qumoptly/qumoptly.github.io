Where to Buy Distributed Feedback Lasers

Definition: lasers where the whole laser resonator consists of a periodic structure, in which Bragg reflection occurs

See also our encyclopedia article on distributed feedback lasers!

Related products: distributed Bragg reflector lasers, laser diodes, fiber lasers
