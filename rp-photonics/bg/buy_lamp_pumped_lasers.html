Where to Buy Lamp-pumped Lasers and Replacement Lamps

Definition: lasers which are pumped with flash lamps or arc lamps, or in rare cases with tungsten halogen lamps

See also our encyclopedia article on lamp-pumped lasers!

Related products: gas discharge lamps, hollow cathode lamps, flash lamps, pump chambers, solid-state lasers
