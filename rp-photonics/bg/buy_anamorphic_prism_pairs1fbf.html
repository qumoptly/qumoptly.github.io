Diode lasers usually feature an elliptical beam proﬁle. Often, however, a circular beam shape is required, e.g. for mode-matching to an external resonator, adapting beam size to pass a small aperture of an isolator, or to obtain a supreme ﬁber coupling efﬁciency. The Anamorphic Prism Pairs J is an adjustable anamorphic prism pair which circularizes an elliptical beam, by either expanding or compressing one of the beam axes.
