Where to Buy Dielectric Coatings

Definition: thin-film coatings made of transparent dielectric materials, e.g. for laser mirrors or anti-reflection coatings

See also our encyclopedia article on dielectric coatings!

Related products: dielectric mirrors, anti-reflection coatings, thin-film production equipment

Check List for Dielectric Coatings

Click here to see a check list before buying such products!
  • Does the coating have the desired reflectivities at the relevant wavelength – for some range of wavelengths, temperatures and angles of incidence?
  • In case of difficult substrates (e.g., very small end faces, strongly curved faces): will the coating safely stick to the surface?
  • If the substrate will be subject to large temperature variation (e.g., a nonlinear crystal heated in a crystal oven): are the thermal expansion coefficients of substrate and coating sufficiently similar? (Problems can occur e.g. for crystals with anisotropic expansion.)
  • For used with pulsed lasers: is the optical damage threshold of the coating sufficiently high?

In case that you need additional advice, consultancy from RP Photonics may be the solution.
