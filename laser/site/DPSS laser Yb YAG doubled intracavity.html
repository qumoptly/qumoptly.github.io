DPSS laser Yb: YAG doubled intracavity



       
 


FREQUENCY DUPLICATION WITH INTRACAVITY KTP

This DPSS laser pumped by a 17W fiber diode goes to a laser head.
This laser head consists of a coupler for the fiber with a fiber exit lens followed by a 45 degree mirror and then a focusing lens that allows to couple the energy of the diode to the YAG bar of size 3 x 10mm including a mirror HR 1064 / HT 808 on its input side and on the output side a HR 808 / HT 1064 mirror.
Then the beam passes a polarizer then a diaphragm and enters the resonant cavity of the KTP.
This cavity is outside the YAG cavity.
This cavness KTP consists of 2 spherical mirrors with a radius of curvature equal to the distance between the 2 mirrors which allows to have a maximum intensity at the common focus of these 2 mirrors focus which is in the center KTP crystal.
The orientation in the 3 axes is realized by external screws.
The KTP input mirror is HT 1064 HR 532 with 140mm bend radius and the HR 1064 HT 532 output mirror, the 2 mirrors are adjustable in X and Y.
The KTP is 3 X 3 X 5 mm and is trailed AR 532 and 1064 nm.
