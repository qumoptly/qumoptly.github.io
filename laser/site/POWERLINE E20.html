POWERLINE E20



Lhas series of lasers POWERLINE E of ROFIN-SINAR home now COHERENT are lasers used to etch materials of all kinds.
This laser consists of a Nd: YVO4 crystal pumped longitudinally by one side in the optical axis. It includes an acousto-optical modulator for working in triggered mode (q-switch) as well as a 12.5 X telescope to reduce the divergence of the output beam. It is also equipped with a frequency doubler (532nm) or even a tripler (355nm)
It can work continuously (CW) or pulsed between 1 and 200kHz with pulse duration of 15 to 400 ns.
