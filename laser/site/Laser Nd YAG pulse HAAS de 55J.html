HAJ pulsed Nd: YAG laser from 55J



LASER HAAS LAG 15

I bought this laser Haas LAG 15 on Ebay for 400 euro!

The power supply consists of a thyristor-controlled load unit.

This power supply is adjustable between 800 volts and 1600 volts.

An LC-type current generator can generate current pulses from 1.25 ms to 10 ms by means of capacitors and chokes in series.

The maximum energy is 3840 Joules at 2 kV for 10 ms or a peak current of 576 A and 128 A rms.

It also consists of a cooling unit by circulating deionized water in a closed circuit cooled by a refrigeration system (Freon)




LC generator composed of abilities and selfs  

This laser allows drilling and welding according to the programmed pulse duration.

The duration of pulse depends on the duration of the flash flash which depends on the capacity and the self of the discharge circuit (LC).

The shortest duration is 1.25 ms is achieved by discharging a capacity of 240 uF with a choke of 1 mH.

To obtain a pulse of 10 ms we have a capacity of 1920 uF and a self of 8 mH!

The energy increases as the duration of the pulse increases. Long pulse times are used for spot welding and the short duration to be pierced.
