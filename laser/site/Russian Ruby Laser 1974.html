Russian Ruby Laser 1974



RUBY LASER MANUFACTURING RUSSIA 1974

 This laser is of Russian origin and dates from 1974. It was given to me by a Russian friend for my collection. When I opened the packet the ruby ​​of diameter 7mm x 120mm fell to the ground and broke! Pity !
The flash tube is also Russian made it is 8mm in diameter by 120mm arc length. The cavity is cooled by circulation of demineralised water. The flash tube is triggered by a trigger coil.
