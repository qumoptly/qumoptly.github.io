Femto amplifier SPITFIRE



 Here is the titanium-sapphire found in the SPITFIRE ACE-35 laser

       

I bought it on Ebay USA in 2009! A good deal!!!!  




The pulse of 35 femtoseconds generated by the TSUNAMI oscillator laser is injected into the SPITFIRE PRO regenerative preamplifier, the pumping of Ti: Sapphire is done by a laser frequency doubled EMPOWER 45 with a repetition rate of 1kHZ.
One day remains to make an assembly of this bar. As an indication, the price of a SPITFIRE PRO is close to one million euros! ! ! I hope someday find a preamp system on Ebay for a few thousand euros!
