Mon premier DPSS 532 nm










 DPSS  YAG + KTP
This laser consists of an arm containing the Cr: Nd: YAG laser pumped by 3 laser diodes of 40W each producing a single-mode beam of 25W at 1064nm and a second arm equipped with a KTP crystal to produce the doubling of frequency. The HR 1064 and HT 532 mirror has a radius of curvature of 300 mm so it focuses the IR radiation into the doubler crystal. The calculated output power at 532 nm should be 12W! Currently I only go out 1.3W because the KTP is of poor quality (bought on Ebay for $ 20)
