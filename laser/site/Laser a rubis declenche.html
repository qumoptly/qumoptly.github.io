Ruby Laser Triggered



LASER  A RUBIS  Q-switch

This ruby ​​laser is triggered by a Pockel cell. The operating principle is as follows: the resonator consists of 1 HR @ 694nm mirrors and a 60% reflectivity HC mirror, between these 2 mirrors is the ruby ​​bar, a polarizer and a Pockel cell in KDDP. During the pumping of the ruby ​​by the flash tube a voltage V 1/4 is applied on the pockel cell and when the population inversion is maximum the voltage is cut and the giant pulse is formed.




Another variant with 2 HR mirrors and the output is on the polarization plate
