20W fiber laser SLD INC model FL20



Here is a 20W HE11 fiber laser.
  The Ytterbium fiber laser has 2 enormous advantages, ie this laser is a 2-level laser with a doping up to 10% and a long radiative life which allows get high pulsed energy and high power output and quantum efficiency close to 92%! The pump laser diode having a yield close to 50% which gives an elecric-laser conversion efficiency of 46%.
Another advantage is the good heat dissipation due to the small diameter of the fiber and its great length so a volume ratio pumped dissipation surface very large.
