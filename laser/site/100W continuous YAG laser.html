100W continuous YAG laser



    LASER YAG:Nd 100W cw
This YAG laser doped with 1% Nodymium is a continuous laser. It is pumped by a 3000W Krypton lamp. The lamp and the YAG bar are cooled by deionized water. A circuit with a circulation pump a liquid-liquid heat exchanger as well as a resin cartridge used to maintain a conductivity lower than 10 micro Siemens. This laser comes from a medical laser that I reassembled fully and will be tested soon! It has an output coupled on an optical fiber, it was surely a laser scalpel!
