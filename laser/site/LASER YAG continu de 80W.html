80W YAG LASER Continuous



YAG Laser 80 W CW
This laser consists of a former YAG medical laser head with a power supply of 6 kW for a Krypton arc tube for pumped YAG.

The cooling circuit consists of a high-pressure centrifugal pump provided with the necessary safety for the operation of the laser, a deionisation cartridge mounted with a bypass and a tank as well as a particle filter and a liquid / liquid heat exchanger.

The output is on a 50 micron optical fiber
