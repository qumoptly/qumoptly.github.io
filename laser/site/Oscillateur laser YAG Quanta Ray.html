Quanta Ray YAG Laser Oscillator



YAG laser: Nd pumped by 2 flash lamp in series in a double ellipse cavity. The YAG and the flashlamps are cooled with a deionized water circuit. This laser gives 450 mJ at 20 pulses per second. In another experiment I add a same module to double the output energy. (+ oscillator amplifier); To simplify the tuning of the resonator, I use a retro reflector (3-sided prism)
