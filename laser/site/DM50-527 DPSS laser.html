DM50-527 DPSS laser




This laser is used to pump a titanium sapphire amplifier. It transmits at 527nm 50mJ for 150ns and at a repetition rate of 1kHz.
The company PHOTONICS INDUSTRIES holds patents for doubling, tripling and quadruple frequency intra-cavity!
I bought it by a demolition company for a pittance! After a first inspection I found a doubler crystal damaged, the cooling circuit of the acousto-optic cell non-functional!
As can be seen in the picture below, there was a coolant leak in this laser head. This laser costs 181'000 Euro TTC so it is worth repairing!



The laser head consists of a neodymium-doped YLF bar pumped by 3 500W diode laser diode arrays with circulating water cooling.
An acousto-optic cell to work in Q-switching and an intra-cavity frequency doubler (LBO mounted on a Peltier cell) This laser will pump dyes at 1kHz!
