Alcon DPSS YAG doubled 3W



This laser consists of a crystal YAG: Nd and a frequency doubler with a crystal KTP and work in intracavity. Output power in 532nm and maximum 3W.
This ALCON laser is an ophthalmic laser used in the treatment of retinal detachment at the back of the eye.
