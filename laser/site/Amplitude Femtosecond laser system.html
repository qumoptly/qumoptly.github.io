Amplitude Femtosecond laser system



This Ytterbium fiber laser produces pulses of 1000fs with 3nJ energy at a repetition frequency of 20MHz! I buy it on Ebay! Currently I have not found any documentation on this laser ...
I am going to use it to make experiments of intensity autocorrelation by fluorescence with 2 photons for the measurement of the duration of pulse to see http://www.swissrocketman.fr/autocorrelation-2-photons,fr,8,128.cfm

as well as measurements of pulse duration by multi-shot interferometric optical autocorrelation, using a GaP photodiode in 2-photon absorption regime .
.
http://www.swissrocketman.fr/realisation-autocorelateur,fr,8,127.cfm



This laser is no longer in the sales program of Amplitude Systeme, it has been replaced by a more powerful model the MIKAN 20nJ 200fs laser!
http://www.amplitude-systemes.com/?lang=2
http://www.amplitude-systemes.com/client/document/mikan_4.pdf

The first experiment is to check if the laser is working well in locked modes. The pulse duration is 1ps and the detector THORLABS DET025A (Si photodiode) has a bandwidth of 2GHz (rise time 150ps), so it does not allow to characterize the duration. pulse, on the other hand to check if the laser works in rpm impulse. The repetition frequency is 40MHz!
