Spectra Physics TSUNAMI picoseond green







This laser is a special version of the Spectra Physics TSUNAMI picosecond equipped with 2 crystals of vanadate doped with neodymium and pumped by an 808nm diode laser.

IR pulses of 2ps are doubled in a BBO and the output is at 532nm. Which is exactly the opposite with the TSUNAMI picosecond or femtosecond with a sapphire doped with titanium.
