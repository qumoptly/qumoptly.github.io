Ytterbium fiber laser doubled in a PPLN



FITEL's Ytterbium fiber laser is composed of:

A pump laser diode working at 976 nm which is centered on the absorption band of silica fiber doped with Ytterbium and emitting at 1100 and 1122nm.

A silica fiber doped with 10% Ytterbium Yb ion composed of a double "cladding" this fiber measures about ten meters in length.

A Periodically Polished Lithium Niobate (PPLN) which is a non-linear effect crystal that converts one frequency into another. (in this case a doubling) which gives 555 and 561nm (green)
