My first fiber laser



This is the mounting of my first fiber laser.
The goal is to gain some experience with this kind of laser by learning to realize fiber couplers for later if the results are positive, realize a fiber laser to coupled modes for obtaining femtosecond pulses.

The laser consists of a 130W fiber-optic diode pump laser at 975nm that focuses on the peak absorption of Ytterbium-doped glass. The fiber is provided with an external mirror HR and an output mirror having a transmission of 50%.
