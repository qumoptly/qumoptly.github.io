Nanolaser DPSS tripled




             JDSU Nanolaser is composed of a microchip cavity consisting of a layer of Cr +4 saturable YAG absorbed embedditholithically in a YAG: Nd +3 laser crystal pumped with a pulsed laser diode at 808 nm. The output IR pulse at 1062 nm is triplet in 2 BBO non linear crystal to obtain UV pulse at 355 nm. With 500 picosecond and 20 kW peak power at 1000 pps repetition rate.
Application: LIDAR, LIBS, Fluorescence, material processing, micro marking MALDI-TOF for Genomics
