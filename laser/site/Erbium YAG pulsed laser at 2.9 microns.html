Erbium YAG pulsed laser at 2.9 microns



The performance of YAG: Erbium lasers is not very spectacular in terms of efficiency and output energy.

The interest of the erbium laser resides in the emission wavelength which is at 2940nm. This wavelength is strongly absorbed by the water which can therefore be used in medicine in plastic surgery.
The YAG: Er bar is doped with 50% erbium ions, the pumping can be carried out with a flash tube (0.16% yield) or QCW laser diode with a yield of 14%. It's a 4-level system. This laser comes out 1000 mJ with 800J of flash pump in 250 microseconds
