086 Picosecond Ruby TDL



PICOSECOND THIN DISK RUBY LASER

PAGE AND LASER UNDER CONSTRUCTION!

I am going to build arubylasercomposed of a 0.08% Cr+3dopedruby discpumped by a triggered YAG laser. and doubled in frequency.
The goal is to obtain a picosecond emission in the wavelength of 694nm.
Blocking of the longitudinal modes will be done in a first phase with a saturable dye such as cryptocyanine or Malachite green. Another possibility is to use a SESAM mirror.
The ruby ​​disk will have a diameter of 13mm and a thickness of 3mm with a doping between 0.08 and 0.1% of Cr2O3. The reverse side of the ruby ​​will have a multi-dielectric layer HR 99.8% at 532 and 694nm and the front face an anti-reflection AR for 532 and 694nm.
As the repetition frequency will be of the order of 40 pulses per second and the pumping is at 532nm (monochromatic) the heating can be controlled by means of a copper support cooled with a Elementary Pelletier.
