EMPOWER 45 DPSS high energy laser



 
The Empower® series is the next generation of high power pulsed green solid state lasers from Spectra-Physics.

These Q-switched Nd: YLF lasers deliver an average output power of up to 45 W at 527 nm and pulse repetition rates from 1 kHz to 10 kHz.

Ideal for pumping: Ti: Sapphire amplifiers such as the Spectra-Physics Spitfire® series, the Empower pulse energies are as high as 28 mJ at 1kHz



The laser head consists of a 5mm diameter, 100mm long YLF bar doped with NORTHROP GRUMMAN's 160W cw (Cutting Edge Optronics) diodes.
The crystal doubler of frequency is of the intra-cavity type, the cavity being formed of the mirror M1 R max 99.8% @ 1.047 and the mirror M5 R max 99.5% @ 1.047 and R max 99.95% @ 527nm .
The output mirror R s = 96% is directly deposited on the LBO doubler crystal on the other side of the LBO is cut to the Brewster incidence.
Mirrors M2 and M3 are R max@ 1.047 and only serve to return the beam output in the AOM2 acousto-optic modulator which is cut on both sides at Brewster incidence.
The M4 mirror at 45 ° is R max @ 1.047 and AR / AR @ 527nm. The MS blade takes 0.01% of the light doubled for laser regulation. SP is an intra-cavity shutter used to stop laser emission.
AOM1 is an acousto-optic modulator serving as Q-switching transforming the continuous beam into a pulsed beam whose pulse duration is 150ns at a repetition rate of 1Hz to 10kHz.
