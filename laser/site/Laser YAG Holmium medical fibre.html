YAG laser: Holmium medical fiber



The YAG: Cr: Tm: Ho laser is a 4-level laser delivering pulses of 0.2-3.5J at 2100nm with a repetition frequency of 5Hz-50Hz. This VERSAPULSE medical laser was used to destroy kidney stones because it creates acoustic shock waves in liquid media.
