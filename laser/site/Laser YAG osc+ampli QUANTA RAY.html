Laser YAG osc+ampli QUANTA RAY




     YAG:Nd oscillator and amplificator
             QUANTA RAY module.

In Nd :YAG amplifier spontaneous emission and self-oscillation due to the high gain properties of Nd:YAG effectively limit the energy storage density in the rod and therefore the useful energy which can be extracted. In Nd:YAG the energy storage factor b = 4.73/J a value 30 times higher than that of Nd:Glass and 105 times higher than that of a ruby rod.

As a result of the high gain in Nd:YAG , only small inversion level can be achieved
