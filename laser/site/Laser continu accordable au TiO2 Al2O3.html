TiO2 tunable continuous laser: Al2O3



This laser consists of a sapphire crystal doped with titanium to obtain a continuous emission between 675 and 1100nm.
It consists of a resonator in which is located sapphire water-cooled titanium crystal and a Lyot filter to select the laser emission frequency with a fineness of 5GHz.
The crystal is pumped by a 5W argon ion laser.
