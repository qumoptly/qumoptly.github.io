Picosecond Yb:YAG laser DPSS



M-PICO  LASER   Ytterbium : YAG


This laser was developed by the company MONTFORT Laser GmbH founded in 2011 by Dr. Daniel Kopf, founder and CEO of the company HIGH Q GmbH acquired by NEWPORT Co.
The laser is based on a 0.1% YAG doped Ytterbium crystal sandwiched between a SESAM mirror-based resonator to block the longitudinal modes and an OC exit mirror that has passed through. The pump beam from a 60W laser diode at 980nm and having a reflection coefficient of 90% for 1030nm wavelength of the picosecond laser.
The laser diode pump is external to the laser housing and is brought by an optical fiber on the laser head.
