10W DPSS cw laser MILLENNIA



This laser is pumped by two 25W laser diodes contained in the 19 "power rack.Two fiber optics brings the pump energy to the laser head.This laser boldly replaces a 10W argon ion laser. consume 20 times less electricity and have a lifespan 50 times higher!






The pump energy of the Nd: YVO4 is fed by 2 optical fibers from the power supply on which are mounted the 2 laser diodes.
This pumping is carried out by means of 2 telescopes focusing the pump beam by the 2 opposite faces of the crystal.
The resonator consists of a mirror M1 HR with 2 dichroic mirrors M2 and M3, a diaphragm, a mirror M4 HR concave, a mirror M5 output RH 1064nm and OC 95% 532nm, of a focusing lens L1 for focusing the pump energy in the doubler crystal LBO and mirror M6 HR 1064 and HR 532nm.

The output is made through a polarization plate and a telescope allowing a minimum divergence.
