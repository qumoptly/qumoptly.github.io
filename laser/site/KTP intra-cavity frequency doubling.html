KTP intra-cavity frequency doubling



 

 This laser head consists of a coupler for the fiber with a fiber exit lens followed by a 45 degree mirror and then a focusing lens that allows to couple the energy of the diode to the YAG bar of dimension 3 x 10mm comprising on its input side a mirror HR 1064 / HT 808 and on the output side a mirror HR 808 / HT 1064.
Then the beam passes a polarizer then a diaphragm and enters the resonant cavity of the KTP. This cavity is outside the YAG cavity. This cavness KTP consists of 2 spherical mirrors with a radius of curvature equal to the distance between the 2 mirrors which allows to have a maximum intensity at the common focus of these 2 mirrors focus which is in the center KTP crystal. The orientation in the 3 axes is realized by external screws.
The KTP input mirror is HT 1064 HR 532 with 140mm bend radius and the HR 1064 HT 532 output mirror, the 2 mirrors are adjustable in X and Y. The KTP is 3 X 3 X 5 mm and is traced AR 532 and 104 nm.
