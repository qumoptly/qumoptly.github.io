SAGITTA 60 DPSS Q-switch YAG laser



Here is a DPSS laser pumped longitudinally by one of the 2 faces of the Nd: YAG crystal.
The laser cavity is formed by a totally reflective mirror for the emission wavelength and totally transparent for the pump wave.
This mirror is directly deposited on one of the faces of the crystal, the other side is AR for the emission length and totally HR reflector for the pump wave.
The output mirror has a reflectivity of 90% and is concave which forms a hemispherical resonator.

 

The pump laser diode is composed of 4 diodes of 50W each. The output beam is directed on an aspheric lens which focuses the pump light by one of the faces of the YAG bar towards the axis of the bar. This type of pumping is the "longitudinal pumping" The laser diode is broken as well as the correction lens mounted on the pump diode. For $ 280 we can not ask for more!
