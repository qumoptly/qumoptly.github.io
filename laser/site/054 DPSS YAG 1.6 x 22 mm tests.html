DPSS YAG 1.6 x 22 mm tests





The goal is to do on my bench of optical tests of operation in different regimes which are:

CWwork continuously with a simple resistor M1 99.85% @ 1060nm and M2 90% @ 1060nm

QCW work in rd Pulsed pulse by pulsing the pump diode. M1 and M2 as with continuous pumping. (1us - 1ms)

Q-switch MR work in regime triggered with a rotating prism and M2 90% (100 ns)

Q-switch AOM work in regime triggered with an acousto-optic modulator ( 20 ns)

Q-switch AS working in a regime triggered with a saturable absorber cell (1 -5 ns)

Q-switch PML Blocking modes with a saturable absorber cell (10-20 ps)

Q-switch AM Mode blocking with amplitude modulated q-switch (10 ps)



  Construction of a small DPSS with a Nd: YAG bar of 1.6mm x 22mm co-doped at 0.5% Nd and 0.5% Cr.
The pump diode is a COHERENT of 20W bought on Ebay for 25 euro but that leaves only 6.5W cw.
A TEC with a regulation is mounted on a copper cooling block with a fan.
