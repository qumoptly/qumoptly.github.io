Yb:YAG laser continu



Ytterbium YAG laser pumped by laser diode

The Yb: YAG laser can produce high power pulses because it has a very long excited state of the state (1 ms) and can be boosted up to 20% in Yb ions. It has an absorption band of 18 nm or 10X more than the Nd: YAG!
The emission is tunable between 1018 and 1053 nm. The quantum yield of Yb: YAG is close to 91%!
The laser diode that I use to pump this crystal is 500W qcw at 941 nm.
