Compact 2W Green DPSS LASER



Ultra-compact DPSS laser doubled frequency by means of a KTP crystal equipped with a heating resistor with a temperature sensor to achieve the phase agreement.
The laser crystal Nd: YAG 2x2x10mm3 is pumped by a laser diode from 20W to 808nm.
