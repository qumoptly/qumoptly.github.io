Femto HighQ



This femtosecond laser is the model IC1040-3000

consists of a crystal Yb: SYS pumped by a laser diode of 8W @ 979nm. It emits light pulses of 568kW for 250 femtoseconds and this at a replay frequency of 21MHz! The beam is TEM00 of 1mm in diameter and at 1040 nm. The energy contained in a pulse is 142 nJ.

 

an 8W laser diode emits through a 6X telescope and is then focused by a doublet on the Yb: SYS crystal whose resonator consists of 2 concave dichroic mirrors, a SESAM mirror serving to phase the different longitudinal frequencies to create the femtosecond pulse (SPM) The repetition frequency depends on the total length of the optical path between the SESAM and the coupler 2% output.
The 2 prisms in (SF10) are used for group speed compensation (GVD). Locking of the longitudinal modes locked in phase is done by the SESAM mirror.
The slot serves as spectral filtering of the gain so that the laser starts in femtosecond impulse regime and not in continuous rpm.
