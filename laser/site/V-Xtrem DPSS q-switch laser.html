V-Xtrem DPSS q-switch laser


This laser gives 7W at 50 kHz which gives pulses of 140 μJ during 40ns or a peak power of 3500W peak focused on a surface of 4 μm 2 gives a flux of 87 GW / cm 2. The same laser working continuously gives a flow of 175MW / cm2.
One of the advantages of the vanadate is that it can be pumped between 801 and 821nm with a maximum absorption at 809nm. the emission is at 1064 nm and the fluorescence time is 97 micro seconds.
