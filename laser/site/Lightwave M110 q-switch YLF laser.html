Lightwave M110 q-switch YLF laser



The LIGHTWAVE ELECTRONICS LWE-110 laser is a laser with a YFF doped 0.2% Nd2O3 crystal pumped at 795 nm.

It can reach 1047nm in TEM00. Two operating regimes can be selected either continuous CW or pulsed nanosecond with an intra-cavity acousto-optical modulator.

The pulse duration is 7ns with a repetition frequency adjustable between 0-50kHz.

The pulse energy depends on the repetition frequency and varies from 10 microjoules at 30kHz up to 70 microjoules at 1kHz.

The diameter of the beam at the exit is 0.2mm with a divergence of 8 mrad.

As an option the laser can be equipped with a 10X telescope, which makes it possible to have a beam of 2mm diameter with 0.8 mrad. Another option is a frequency doubler that allows an emission at 523.5nm.




The output energy according to the repetition frequency. It can be seen that the nettle energy of Nd: YLF is double compared to Nd: YAG.

Up to 1 kHz the energy is 70 microjoule then it is only 10 microjoule 30 kHz.
