Rofin Sinar 550W DPSS head



The ROFIN DY022 laser with an output power of 2200W is composed of 4 heads mounted in series to prevent the laser bars from going super radiance due to the very high gain of the Nd: YAG.
Each head consists of a YAG bar doped with neodymium 7mm diameter and 170mm long pumped by 40 diodes 50W unit or 2.4 kW @ 808nm.
A module comes out 550W at 1.062nm with a merit factor of 12mm x mrad. The DY022 laser costs more than $ 400,000 , which means that the laser head costs at least $ 70,000! I bought it on eBay 300 euro! With this laser head there is the possibility to work also in regime triggered with an electro-optical modulator and to make doubling of frequency intra and extracavity with a power of output of 300W in the green (532nm).
