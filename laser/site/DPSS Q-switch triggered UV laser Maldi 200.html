DPSS Q-switch triggered UV laser Maldi 200



Another good deal on Ebay. (80 euro for this jewelery!)

This laser is a frequency tripled YAG DPSS q-switch pumped by a QCW laser diode of 100W or CW of 30W. The laser is complete it lacks the pumping diode at 808nm for optical fiber coupling.

This laser consists of a QCW or CW pump laser diode attached to an optical fiber that carries the pump energy at 808nm over the YAG bar mounted in a copper block to dissipate heat from the bar. A set of lenses used to concentrate the radiation in the YAG: Nd. One face of the bar is provided with a multi-dielectric deposit Rmax for 1064nm and AR for 808nm the other side is AR 1064 and Rmax 808nm. Then a polarizer and a pockel cell can trigger the laser to obtain giant pulses (Q-switch). A 90% R exit mirror completes the resonant cavity of the laser. Next come a SHG (frequency doubler) then a THG (frequency tripler) which allows to obtain a pulsed monomode laser emission of 7ns and 200kW peak at 1000 pps. After the tripling of frequency we find a telescope to increase the diameter of the beam and decrease the divergence. In output there are still 2 filters to eliminate 532 and 1064 nm.
