FEMTO LEXEL810



The Lexel model 810 modelocked Ti:sapphire laser is a commercial ultrafast pulsed laser.

It is compact in size, efficient and has options including CW, picosecond, and femtosecond operations installed in the same unit.

The pulsed operations can be initiated with standard AO modelocker, with passive semiconductor saturable absorber, or just with free-run (femtosecond operation).

The adjustable femtosecond pulse width and the wide wavelength tunable range allow fine tuning of the laser to meet the needs of the application. Two sets of optics are used. The advanced cavity design results in a stable and low-noise laser performance for many days even in free-run operation. Other benefits include temperature stabilized TE cooling, sealed and N2 purged laser case design for ultra stable operation, and quick switching between CW, picosecond, and femtosecond operations. The laser can accept any power from 4-10 watts of multiline argon or green DPSS lasers while maintaining a guaranteed peak efficiency rate of 12%.
