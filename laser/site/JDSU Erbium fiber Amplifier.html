JDSU Erbium fiber Amplifier



Here is a laser amplifier composed of 14 fiber laser diodes and 3 erbium doped fibers for amplifying a laser signal. This type of amplifier is found on intercontinental communication fiber optic lines every 80km to amplify the laser signal.
I buy it on Ebay for 35 euro! The goal is to recover fibred laser diodes, Erbium fibers and WDM coupler to one day realize a femtosecond laser fiber.
For this it will be necessary to find a machine to weld the monomode fibers between them.  


The operating principle is as follows:
an erbium doped amplifying optical fiber receives a low intensity optical signal which is amplified by means of a pump laser beam generated by a laser diode.
The pump beam creates a positive gain in the fiber which amplifies the weak signal that appears to amplify.
