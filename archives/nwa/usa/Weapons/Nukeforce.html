The nuclear forces listed above are the current active duty (combat coded) forces and force loadings. It represents the weapons that can be launched all at once, with no more than a few hours notice. The U.S. arsenal of operation warheads is larger than this, and the total inventory of warheads is larger still (see U.S. Nuclear Weapon Enduring Stockpile).
